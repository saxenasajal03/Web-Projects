19+ Years of Scaling The Zenith Of Excellence

Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions (DBGI) ,which is now Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University (DBUU), first opened its gate in 2005 intending to provide excellent quality of education that molds students and makes them stand as true professionals in real life. Here at DBGI, we ensure to impart value-based education to the students. As an institution, it is our responsibility to uphold and strengthen our society by preparing aware, skilled, and wise decision-makers of tomorrow. Our uprightness is our driving force, and we swear by the commitment to provide opportunities from every nook and corner for our student’s professional and personal development so that nothing can stop them from living a prosperous life. With the kind of integrity that DBGI upholds it has become the Best Engineering College in Dehradun.

Awarded for Best Placement College in Dehradun, Uttarakhand By MHRD

Being the Best Engineering college in Dehradun encompasses a blend of a vibrant array of cultures, opinions, and unparalleled prospects that collectively make DBGI a splendid place to study, nurture, and grow. More than 55 Under Graduate and Post Graduate programs with the best faculty give students the edge in their careers. The programs comprise a wide spectrum: Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, Commerce, Hotel Management, Ayurveda, Architecture, Design (Graphic, Interior, Product), Mass Communication, Fashion Designing, Polytechnic, Agriculture, Forestry, Horticulture, Microbiology, Tourism, Hospital administration, Nursing, and Pharmacy. The strong foundation of DBGI has been setting a benchmark and has greatly helped in maintaining its position as the top engineering college in Uttarakhand.
